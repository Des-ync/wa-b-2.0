Frequently asked.

How is billing actually charged?
A Hubtel MoMo prompt is sent to your registered number on the 1st of every month. Approve on your phone. That's it. We never store your MoMo PIN.
What if a payment fails?
We retry with exponential backoff (8 attempts). You get a WhatsApp reminder 3 days before billing, on the day, and after each failure. Your service stays on for 3 grace days, then suspends. You can pay any time by texting PAY.
Can I switch plans?
Yes: text UPGRADE or DOWNGRADE. Changes are pro-rated to the rest of the period.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fee on any plan. We help you onboard for free during the 14-day trial.
Are transaction fees included?
The plan price is your platform fee. Customer-side payment processing fees (Paystack/Hubtel) are passed through at cost, typically 1.5% for MoMo, 2.95% for cards in Ghana.
Do you offer NGO or student pricing?
Yes: registered Ghanaian NGOs and student-run businesses get 50% off any plan. Email hello@wa-b.com with your registration document.

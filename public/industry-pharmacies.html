For pharmacies & wellness stores

A quieter way to reorder the essentials.

Over-the-counter items, personal care, baby and wellness products — the things customers reorder without wanting a conversation about it. WhatsApp gives them a private, one-on-one thread instead of a public counter.

WA-B handles ordering, catalog, and payment for retail items. It doesn't process prescriptions or replace your pharmacist's professional judgment — that stays exactly where it belongs.

Why WA-B for pharmacies

Private by default. Open when it matters.

A WhatsApp chat is a one-to-one line, not a shop floor. Customers can ask for something quietly and get an answer just as quietly — and your posted business hours mean they know exactly when to expect one.

  • Catalog of over-the-counter and personal care items, by category
  • Low-stock alerts so popular items don't quietly run out
  • MoMo and card checkout, confirmed before the order is packed
  • Business hours set clearly, including after-hours expectations

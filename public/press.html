WA-B Solutions is the automated WhatsApp B2P sales bot for Ghanaian SMEs. The platform turns any WhatsApp number into a 24/7 storefront — customers browse, order, and pay via Mobile Money or card, all inside the chat. WA-B handles the conversation engine, payment reconciliation, order tracking, and monthly subscription billing in cedis. Founded in 2025 in Accra. wa-b.com

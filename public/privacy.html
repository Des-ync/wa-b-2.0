Privacy Policy

Plain-English summary first. Detailed terms below. We don't sell your data, we don't share it across tenants, and we don't read your customers' messages for any purpose other than running your bot.

Last updated: 1 May 2026 · Effective: 1 May 2026

1. Who we are

WA-B Solutions Ltd. ("WA-B", "we", "us") is a company registered in Ghana (Reg. No. CS-000000-2025) at 2nd Floor, Cantonments Office Park, 14 Olusegun Obasanjo Way, Accra. We provide a SaaS platform that lets businesses sell on WhatsApp and accept Mobile Money payments.

2. Data we collect

From merchants (you)

  • Account details: name, business name, email, WhatsApp number, role.
  • Billing details: MoMo phone number, billing history, plan tier.
  • Usage: dashboard activity, API key usage, support requests.

From your customers (end users of your bot)

  • WhatsApp phone number (E.164 format).
  • Display name as set in their WhatsApp profile.
  • Messages exchanged with your bot, plus any details they share (delivery address, order items).
  • Payment metadata: amount, status, timestamp, transaction reference. We never see the MoMo PIN or full card numbers.

3. How we use it

  • To run your bot: respond to messages, take orders, charge MoMo, deliver receipts.
  • To bill your subscription via Hubtel.
  • To provide aggregated, anonymized analytics in your dashboard.
  • To investigate abuse, fraud, or security incidents.
  • To send you product updates (you can opt out any time).

We do not use your customers' data to train AI, sell ads, or enrich third-party profiles.

4. Where it lives

All production data is stored in PostgreSQL clusters in EU-West (Frankfurt) with read replicas in West Africa (Lagos). Encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Backups are encrypted and retained for 30 days.

5. Who we share it with

  • Meta — to send and receive WhatsApp messages on your behalf.
  • Hubtel — to charge MoMo and reconcile payments.
  • Paystack — to charge cards (only if you enable card payments).
  • Cloud infrastructure — Hetzner (Falkenstein, DE) and Cloudflare (CDN).
  • Compelled disclosure — only with a valid Ghanaian court order.

6. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Export all data in your account (CSV / JSON) from the dashboard.
  • Delete your account — irreversibly purges your data within 30 days.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of personal data on behalf of your customers.

Email privacy@wa-b.com for any of these. We respond within 7 business days.

7. Cookies

The dashboard uses one essential cookie for session auth. We use Plausible Analytics (cookieless, EU-hosted) for marketing-site analytics. No third-party trackers, no ad pixels.

8. Retention

Active account data: kept while your account is active. Closed accounts: purged 30 days after closure. Billing records: 7 years (Ghana Revenue Authority requirement).

9. Children

WA-B is a B2B service and not intended for anyone under 18.

10. Changes

If we make material changes, we'll notify you on the dashboard and via email at least 30 days before they take effect.
